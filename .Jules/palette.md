## 2024-06-11 - Menu Bar App UX Feedback
**Learning:** For background or hidden processes (like audio playback from a menu bar app), providing visual feedback (like changing the app title to an icon) and context-aware menu items (like changing "Speak" to "Stop speaking") is critical so users understand the current state and can easily interrupt long-running actions.
**Action:** Always add visual indicators and contextual toggles for async or background actions in menu bar applications.
